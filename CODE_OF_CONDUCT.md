# 行为准则

[English](#english)

## 中文

请以尊重、直接、建设性的方式协作。

### 我们鼓励

- 清晰表达问题与证据
- 讨论方案时聚焦事实
- 尊重不同背景和经验

### 我们不接受

- 人身攻击
- 歧视或骚扰
- 故意提交破坏性内容

## English

Please collaborate with respect, directness, and constructiveness.

### We encourage

- Clear articulation of problems and evidence
- Fact-focused discussions when evaluating solutions
- Respect for different backgrounds and experience levels

### We do not accept

- Personal attacks
- Discrimination or harassment
- Deliberately destructive contributions
